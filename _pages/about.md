## About

I am a Mechanical Engineer currently pursuing a **M.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering – Mechatronics and Robotics at Politecnico di Milano**. My academic background has provided me with a solid foundation in mechanics, automation, control systems, and robotic technologies, with a strong focus on analytical problem solving and system-oriented engineering design.

My main interests lie in the development of **robotic and mechatronic systems**, particularly where **control, automation, simulation, and intelligent decision-making methods** are integrated into structured engineering workflows. I am especially interested in solutions that connect rigorous engineering principles with practical implementation, spanning **mechanical design, dynamic modeling, motion planning, and advanced automation architectures**.

This website presents a selection of my academic and technical work related to **robotics, control, simulation, and intelligent automation**, providing an overview of my background, technical interests, and ongoing development path.

During my studies I also completed an **Erasmus experience in Brussels**, which strengthened my adaptability and international perspective. I am particularly interested in opportunities in **robotics, automation, mechatronic system development, control, and engineering simulation**, where structured engineering approaches can be applied to real-world technological challenges.
