## About Me

I am a Mechanical Engineer who recently completed a **Master’s degree in Mechanical Engineering – Mechatronics and Robotics at Politecnico di Milano**. My academic background provided me with a solid foundation in mechanics, automation, control systems, and robotic technologies, while strengthening my analytical approach to complex engineering problems and system-oriented design.

My main interests lie in the development of **robotic and mechatronic systems**, particularly where **control, automation, simulation, and intelligent decision-making methods** are integrated into structured engineering workflows. I am especially interested in engineering solutions that connect rigorous theoretical principles with practical implementation, from **mechanical design and dynamic modeling to motion planning and advanced automation architectures**.

During my academic path, I also completed an **Erasmus experience in Brussels**, which contributed to developing adaptability, independence, and an international perspective.

This website presents a selection of my academic and technical work related to **robotics, control, simulation, and intelligent automation**, providing a concise overview of my background and technical interests as I begin my professional career in the field of robotics and advanced engineering systems.
